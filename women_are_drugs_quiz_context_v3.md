# Women Are Drugs Quiz Context Pack

## Purpose
This document is the brand-specific quiz context file for the **Women Are Drugs** version of the social quiz app. It is designed to work alongside the existing app requirements and brand replication guide.

Use this file to define the **quiz identity, messaging, scoring, lead-capture context, results, and content structure** for the Women Are Drugs build so the implementation stays aligned with the established architecture.

---

## Brand Meaning
**Women Are Drugs** is not meant in a derogatory or sexualized way.

Within this quiz experience, **D.R.U.G.S.** stands for:

- **Desirable**
- **Resilient**
- **Unique**
- **Gifted**
- **Strong**

The quiz should frame the brand around **admiration for women’s character, rarity, presence, standards, resilience, and depth**.

The experience should feel:

- bold
- premium
- emotionally intelligent
- streetwear-forward
- culturally sharp
- unisex
- respectful
- socially shareable

This quiz is not about objectifying women.
It is about revealing whether the participant is drawn to **surface-level attraction** or to the deeper qualities represented by the brand.

---

## Quiz Objective
Create a high-converting, social-media-first quiz funnel that asks:

# What Are You Really Addicted To?

The quiz should help users discover whether they are drawn to:

- depth
- strength
- rarity
- standards
- resilience
- uniqueness

or mostly drawn to:

- attention
- convenience
- surface-level attraction
- short-term excitement

The tone should feel **bold enough to go viral**, but **elevated enough to protect the brand**.

## Quiz Tone Rules
The copy throughout the app should follow these rules:

1. Keep the language **short, sharp, and emotionally clean**.
2. Make the quiz feel **fast and rewarding**.
3. Keep all copy **unisex**, so men and women both feel invited to take it.
4. Avoid explicit sexual framing.
5. Avoid anything insulting, degrading, or hostile toward women.
6. Keep the brand message tied back to **Desirable, Resilient, Unique, Gifted, Strong**.
7. Create contrast between **surface** and **substance**.
8. Result copy should feel **shareable**, **identity-based**, and **slightly provocative** in a premium way.

## Quiz Structure
- 6 questions
- 4 answers per question
- fixed weighted scoring
- one question at a time
- mobile-first experience
- result is gated behind lead capture
- result should feel personalized and worth sharing

### Scoring model
- **A = 4 points**
- **B = 3 points**
- **C = 2 points**
- **D = 1 point**

### Total score range
- **Minimum:** 6
- **Maximum:** 24

## Quiz Content

### Question 1
**Prompt:**
What pulls you in first about a woman?

**Answers:**
- **A.** Her presence. The way she carries strength without needing to prove it. **(4 points)**
- **B.** Her uniqueness. She feels different from everyone else. **(3 points)**
- **C.** Her beauty and the attention she naturally gets. **(2 points)**
- **D.** The energy of the moment and how easy it feels. **(1 point)**

### Question 2
**Prompt:**
A woman has high standards, strong boundaries, and knows exactly who she is. What is your reaction?

**Answers:**
- **A.** I respect it. That kind of woman stands out for a reason. **(4 points)**
- **B.** I’m intrigued. Confidence like that is hard to ignore. **(3 points)**
- **C.** I like it, but it can feel a little intense. **(2 points)**
- **D.** I would rather deal with someone less complicated. **(1 point)**

### Question 3
**Prompt:**
Which kind of woman stays on your mind the longest?

**Answers:**
- **A.** The one with depth, gifts, ambition, and real substance. **(4 points)**
- **B.** The one who feels rare and impossible to compare. **(3 points)**
- **C.** The one who is exciting, attractive, and fun to be around. **(2 points)**
- **D.** The one who gives me attention when I want it. **(1 point)**

### Question 4
**Prompt:**
When a woman has been through pain but still moves with strength, what does that do for you?

**Answers:**
- **A.** It draws me in more. Resilience makes her even more powerful. **(4 points)**
- **B.** It makes me want to understand her story. **(3 points)**
- **C.** I notice it, but it is not what matters most to me. **(2 points)**
- **D.** I do not really think that deeply about it. **(1 point)**

### Question 5
**Prompt:**
Be honest. What are you really attracted to most?

**Answers:**
- **A.** A woman who is desirable, resilient, unique, gifted, and strong. **(4 points)**
- **B.** A woman with a rare energy that leaves a lasting impression. **(3 points)**
- **C.** A woman who looks good, feels exciting, and keeps things interesting. **(2 points)**
- **D.** A woman who is easy, available, and simple to deal with. **(1 point)**

### Question 6
**Prompt:**
What kind of woman are you actually addicted to?

**Answers:**
- **A.** The kind that makes you respect her, admire her, and level up. **(4 points)**
- **B.** The kind you never fully forget because she was different. **(3 points)**
- **C.** The kind that is thrilling while it lasts. **(2 points)**
- **D.** The kind that fills a moment but does not leave much behind. **(1 point)**

## Lead Gate Context
The lead gate should appear **after the user answers all six questions and before the final result is revealed**.

### Lead gate goal
Convert quiz engagement into a usable lead while preserving momentum.

### Lead fields
Use the same architecture as the base app, but adapt the copy for this brand.

Required or preferred fields:
- first name
- email
- Instagram handle
- phone number optional if already part of the base system

### Recommended lead gate copy
**Headline:** Your result is ready.

**Body:**
Enter your info to unlock your result and get access to future drops, exclusives, and special offers.

### Field copy suggestions
- **First name placeholder:** Your first name
- **Email placeholder:** Your email
- **Instagram placeholder:** Your Instagram @
- **Phone placeholder:** Optional - Add for a free gift 🎁

### Consent line
By submitting, you agree to receive follow-up messages, brand updates, and special offers.

### Lead incentive suggestions

- Unlock your result and get first access to new drops.
- Unlock your result and get access to exclusive offers.
- Unlock your result and stay tapped in for future releases.
